About Dr. Suhani Sharma

Dr. Suhani Sharma is a board-certified maxillofacial surgeon with over 15 years of experience in advanced facial surgery, dental implants, trauma reconstruction, and corrective jaw surgery. Dedicated to providing personalized care, Dr. Sharma combines advanced technology with compassionate treatment to restore both function and aesthetics.

Facial Trauma Reconstruction

Diagnosis and treatment of cysts, tumors, and other oral pathologies with precise biopsy procedures.
